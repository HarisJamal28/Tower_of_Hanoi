Tower of Hanoi

Objective

You have three rods and a set of disks of different sizes that can slide onto any rod. Your goal is to move all the disks from the first rod to the last rod, following these rules:

  • You can only move one disk at a time.
  • Each move consists of taking the upper disk from one of the stacks and placing it on top of another stack or an empty rod.
  • No larger disk may be placed on top of a smaller disk.
